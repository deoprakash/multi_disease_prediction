    Upload medical images for analysis of potential iron deficiency or anemia
  • Cardiovascular Analysis

  • Cancer Analysis

  • Diabetes Risk Prediction
    Evaluate blood sugar levels and assess the risk of diabetes
  • Obesity Risk Prediction
