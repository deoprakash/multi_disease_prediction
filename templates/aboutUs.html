About the Project

A & D Health AI is an innovative platform that leverages Artificial Intelligence to transform healthcare. Our goal is to provide intelligent tools for disease risk prediction, early diagnosis, and decision support for medical professionals. By combining patient health records, symptoms, and imaging, we offer precised and real time health insights to empower both doctors and patients.

We focus on democratizing access to advanced healthcare technologies by creating a user-friendly web interface powered by machine learning and deep learning models. With this Health AI, we aim to improve lives through smarter, faster, and more accessible medical support.
